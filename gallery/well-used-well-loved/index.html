Well Used, Well Loved

January 31, 2019

A community art project that explores age, beauty, attachment and impermanence through a linen dish towel and journaling.

Well Used, Well Loved

Well Used, Well Loved

Hand dyed, woven linen, hand spun paper with text from participants.
6ft by 12ft
$10,000
2015-17
In community art we uncover our common threads as well as those that define our differences. This community cloth is equal and balanced -- where individual threads are all important to the structure.
