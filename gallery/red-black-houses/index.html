Works that explore the metaphor of house and home.

Feeding the Heart

Feeding the Heart

linen, rayon, metallics, handspun paper, photos
26" by 56"
sold
2009
On a cold Saturday morning in early March, I poked around the Wentworth Gardner House with David Leach. There was something about this house that immediately got under my skin. I was captivated by the detail of the wood carving, the artifacts, the stories and Wallace Nutting’s photographs of the women posing in the house. This piece uses several design elements from the house as well as photographs that mimic Nutting’s images.
